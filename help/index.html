Frequently asked questions

What webcams are compatible?

Most external webcams should be compatible. The software uses an implementation of UVC (Usb Video device Class). The software is tested with Logitech Streamcam, Razor Kiyo and some older Logitech webcams

Why are some of the settings for the camera disabled?

The software retrieves a list of UVC features from the camera itself. The features that are reported to support getting and settings values are enabled in the UI. So, it might be the case that your camera doesn't support some of the features (for example focus or auto-focus, or other settings)
